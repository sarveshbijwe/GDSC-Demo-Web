About GDSC

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a Google Developers program designed for university students to acquire mobile and web development skills. The club is inclusive, catering to both newcomers who are starting their development journey and proficient developers aiming to advance their skills. It functions as a creative arena for students to experiment with fresh concepts and collaborate on solutions for mobile and web development challenges.

Learn more

What is The Benefits?

GDSC enhances mobile and web development skills through workshops and resources. Students gain exposure to the latest trends and technologies. GDSC provides a space for teamwork and problem-solving on development challenges. Members connect with professionals and peers, potentially leading to mentorship and career opportunities. Hackathons and workshops enable hands-on learning and project implementation.
